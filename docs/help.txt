================ Haggis Chess Help Menu ===============

  'help'...........................displays this menu
  'display'...................displays the game board
  'save'......................saves your current game
  'load'.....................loads your previous save
  'b2'..................chooses space B2 on the board

  You may only select pieces that belong to you and
  are moveable. You may only select spaces that are
  not occupied, or are occupied by enemy pieces that
  can be captured by your selected piece. Be careful:
  once you have chosen a piece or space, you cannot 
  undo your selection!
